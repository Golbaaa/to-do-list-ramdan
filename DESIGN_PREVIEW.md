## ğŸ¨ Design System

**Colors:**
- Primary: Blue (#2563EB)
- Success: Green (#10B981)
- Warning: Orange (#F59E0B)
- Error: Red (#EF4444)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) + Light Blue Gradient

**Typography:**
- Header: 24px Bold (text-2xl font-bold)
- Title: 18px Semi-bold (text-lg font-semibold)
- Body: 16px Regular (text-base)
- Small: 14px Regular (text-sm)
- Tiny: 12px Regular (text-xs)

**Spacing:**
- Container padding: 24px (p-6)
- Card padding: 16px (p-4)
- Gap between items: 12-16px
- Border radius: 8px (rounded-lg)

**Effects:**
- Shadow: Subtle drop shadow on cards (shadow-sm, shadow-md on hover)
- Transition: Smooth 200ms transitions on hover
- Opacity: 75% opacity on completed tasks
